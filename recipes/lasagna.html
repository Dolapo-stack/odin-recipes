Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made of layers of wide, flat pasta sheets alternated with rich fillings such as seasoned ground meat (often beef or pork), tomato sauce, b√©chamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella. Each layer is carefully stacked and baked to perfection, creating a hearty, flavorful casserole with a golden, bubbling top.

Lasagna is known for its comforting, savory taste and is often served as a main course at gatherings or special meals. Variations can include vegetables, chicken, seafood, or be made entirely vegetarian.
