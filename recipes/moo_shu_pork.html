Moo Shu Pork

Description

Moo Shu Pork is a popular Chinese-American stir-fry dish featuring:

  • Shredded pork (often marinated for flavor)
  • Scrambled eggs
  • Wood ear mushrooms or shiitake mushrooms
  • Cabbage, carrots, and scallions

These ingredients are cooked together in a savory-sweet hoisin and soy sauce blend and traditionally served with thin Mandarin pancakes or flour tortillas and additional hoisin sauce for wrapping. It’s flavorful, tender, and offers a mix of textures—crunchy, soft, and juicy.
