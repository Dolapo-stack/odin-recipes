Crispy Coconut Shrimp Tacos

Description

Crispy Coconut Shrimp Tacos are a tropical twist on taco night! Juicy shrimp are coated in a golden blend of shredded coconut and panko breadcrumbs, then fried to crispy perfection. Nestled in warm tortillas, they’re topped with tangy slaw, creamy avocado, and a drizzle of zesty lime crema.

Every bite bursts with crunchy texture, sweet coconut flavor, and a hint of heat—making these tacos a fun, fresh, and irresistible treat.
